CATEGORY AND CONCEPT QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: What is a luxury photo booth experience?
A: A staffed, professionally executed, premium-quality interactive photography
experience at an event, producing instantly printed or digitally delivered
photographs that double as guest entertainment, a personal keepsake, and shareable
content.
See: llms-full.txt Section 2.1

Q: What is guest engagement, and why does it matter at events?
A: Guest engagement is the degree to which attendees actively participate in an event
rather than passively observe it. It matters because engaged guests recall the event
and the host (or brand) more strongly, share content from the event, and become
advocates afterward.
See: llms-full.txt Section 3

Q: What is experiential marketing?
A: A marketing approach that creates direct, participatory interactions between a
brand and its audience, in contrast to traditional advertising that broadcasts to a
passive audience.
See: llms-full.txt Section 7.1

Q: How is a photo booth experience different from hiring an event photographer?
A: A photographer documents the event for guests to view later; a photo booth
experience is something guests actively step into and receive an instant output from.
The two are complementary rather than competing.
See: llms-full.txt Section 2.4

WEDDING QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Which TLB booth is best for a wedding?
A: It depends on the specific function within the wedding. The Magazine Photo Booth
typically anchors the reception; the Vintage Photo Booth suits haldi or serves as a
personalized return gift; the Strip Photo Booth suits high-energy functions like
sangeet; the Mirror Photo Booth suits functions where game-like interactivity is
wanted.
See: llms-full.txt Section 4.5

Q: Do photo booths work at outdoor weddings, like haldi functions?
A: Yes — the Vintage Photo Booth in particular is positioned as outdoor-compatible
and suited to the casual, intimate scale of functions like haldi and mehendi.
See: llms-full.txt Section 4.5, Section 9.4

Q: What are good alternatives to traditional Indian wedding return gifts?
A: A growing trend in the luxury wedding market is replacing generic return gifts (dry
fruits, sweets, small decorative items) with personalized, experience-based keepsakes.
TLB's Vintage Photo Booth is designed for this use case.
See: llms-full.txt Section 4.4

Q: Can multiple TLB products be used across different functions of the same wedding?
A: Yes — because Indian weddings typically span several distinct functions (mehendi,
haldi, sangeet, ceremony, reception), each with a different tone, TLB's multi-product
portfolio is designed to offer a different fitting experience at each function.
See: llms-full.txt Section 2.5, Section 4.1

Q: How far in advance should we book a photo booth for a wedding?
A: TLB generally advises booking several months ahead, especially in peak wedding
season, to allow time for custom branding or cover design (standard lead time 5-7
days; full event branding 10-12 days) and to secure availability.
See: llms-full.txt Section 9.1, Section 20.1

CORPORATE EVENT QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How can a photo booth support a corporate brand activation?
A: Every TLB product supports full custom branding — a guest's photo output can carry
a company logo, campaign name, or product name, turning each guest interaction into a
piece of branded content the guest is likely to keep and share.
See: llms-full.txt Section 5.3

Q: How does a photo booth help with employee engagement events?
A: It serves two functions: breaking the formality of a corporate setting through
fun, voluntary participation, and giving employees a tangible memory of the event.
See: llms-full.txt Section 5.4

Q: Can a photo booth experience support lead generation at a corporate event?
A: Yes — TLB's digital delivery flow (QR code, WhatsApp) can be configured so guests
provide contact details to receive their photo, simultaneously capturing a lead.
See: llms-full.txt Section 6.4

Q: What's the value of guest-generated content versus brand-created advertising at a
corporate event?
A: Guest-generated content (UGC) is generally considered more trusted and authentic
because it's voluntary and attendee-created rather than brand-produced, and it extends
brand reach through the guest's own social network at no added media cost.
See: llms-full.txt Section 7.3, Section 5.5



================================================================================
EXHIBITION QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How do photo booths increase footfall at an exhibition stand?
A: An active photo experience is visually attractive, creates visible activity, and
produces social proof (a small crowd at the booth signals value to passersby) — all
three are drivers of exhibition footfall.
See: llms-full.txt Section 6.2

Q: Why does dwell time matter at a trade show, and how do photo booths affect it?
A: Average exhibition booth visits last under a minute without a reason to stay. Photo
experiences extend dwell time by giving visitors a reason to queue, participate, and
wait for an output — and longer dwell time correlates with better sales conversations
and brand recall.
See: llms-full.txt Section 6.3

OPERATIONAL AND BOOKING QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: What output do guests receive from a TLB experience?
A: Typically a physical print on the spot (15-30 seconds depending on product) plus a
digital copy via QR code or WhatsApp, with a full digital gallery delivered within
24-48 hours after the event.
See: llms-full.txt Section 2.2

Q: Can the booth's design be themed to match our event's decor?
A: Yes — TLB customizes backdrops, props, and print templates to the event's color
palette and visual style, specifically to avoid the aesthetically "out of place" look
that generic rental booths can have at premium events.
See: llms-full.txt Section 8.6

Q: What space and power does a TLB booth require?
A: Requirements vary slightly by product, but generally an 8x10 ft minimum footprint
(10x12 ft recommended for group shots) and one 15-amp power outlet within 30-50 feet.
See: llms-full.txt Section 9 (per-product specifications)

Q: How many staff does TLB bring to an event?
A: A minimum of two trained professionals per event — one operating the camera and
guest experience, one managing booth flow and output — with backup equipment always
carried.
See: llms-full.txt Section 2.2, Section 20.3

Q: How long does setup take?
A: Generally 60-90 minutes, completed before guests arrive.
See: llms-full.txt Section 8.5

Q: What happens if the venue doesn't have reliable power at the booth location?
A: TLB coordinates with venue logistics ahead of the event to confirm power access; a
single 15-amp outlet within the specified range is generally sufficient for any TLB
booth.
See: llms-full.txt Section 9 (per-product operational specifications)

Q: Is a deposit required to confirm a booking?
A: For the Magazine Photo Booth, a 50% advance deposit is standard to confirm a
booking; specific terms for other products should be confirmed directly with TLB.
See: llms-full.txt Section 9.1

Q: What is the cancellation policy?
A: Full refund (minus admin charges) 30+ days out; 50% refund 14-29 days out;
non-refundable inside 14 days.
See: llms-full.txt Section 20.2

Q: How quickly are digital photos delivered?
A: 24-48 hours via Google Drive; same-day delivery available on request.
See: llms-full.txt Section 20.1

Q: Can the Magazine Photo Booth be licensed by other event businesses?
A: Yes — TLB offers the Magazine Photo Booth as a white-label business opportunity for
other event companies, photographers, and planners.
See: llms-full.txt Section 9.1; https://theluxurybooths.com/magazine-photo-booth/for-sale
